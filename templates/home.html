Hi! My name is Antoni and this is my DIY frutiger aero style website. I'm a Modern Technologies in Forensic Science student. I'm interested in GNU/Linux and cybersecurity. I also like being tedious how old internet was the best: no AI slop, no paywalls, no ad-trackers on every corner; just find what you need and enjoy yourself in peace.

This is not a blog nor my portfolio (or maybe it is, idk). The purpose of this site is pretty simple: I love 2000s technology. Switching from modern devices packed with AI and other data stealing tools to my good, old blackberry is a great way to rest and slow down. Unfortunately you can't do a lot, as many modern websites or applications are just not compatible with old school devices.

That's why I wanted to create a space, where I could store and create stuff that might come in handy and is available on everything I use. Of course it may look inelegant and be a little sluggish, but nothing beats good old HTML + CSS. No javascript nor modern UI libraries, just some ugly but reliable, spaghetti style.css <3
