Hi! My name is Antoni and this is my frutiger aero styled website. I'm a Modern Technologies in Forensic Science student. I'm interested in GNU/Linux and cybersecurity. I also like being tedious how old internet was the best: no AI slop, no paywalls, no ad-trackers on every corner; just find what you need and enjoy yourself in peace. I like learning new languages (real life languages), that's why you may find some japanese/russian accents.

In the past, I used to work on both frontend (React.js) and backend (Python, tRPC, PostgreSQL), but I have to admit that tinkering with systems and analyzing logs sounds much more fun. Google wouldn't be Google without stable, safe servers and setting them up? That's the good stuff. If you want to check out my projects, feel free to visit my GitHub @LeBulbasaur.

This is not a blog nor my portfolio (or maybe it is, idk). The purpose of this website is pretty simple: I love 2000s technology. Switching from modern devices packed with AI and other data stealing tools to my good, old blackberry is a great way to rest and slow down. Unfortunately you can't do a lot, as many modern websites or applications are not compatible with old school technology.

That's why I wanted to create a space, where I could store and create stuff that might come in handy and is available on everything I use. Of course it may look inelegant and be a little sluggish, but nothing beats good old HTML + CSS. No javascript nor modern UI libraries, just some ugly but reliable, spaghetti style.css <3
